# Customer Journey Map

## Aware
**Trigger Event:** Military or defense organization identifies the need for GPS-independent drone targeting solutions.
**Friction Points:**
- Lack of awareness about alternative navigation methods.
- Limited knowledge about the capabilities of laser-guidance or inertial measurement units.
**User Emotions:** Frustration, uncertainty, curiosity.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Educational content (blogs, whitepapers) explaining the benefits of laser-guidance and inertial measurement units.
- Case studies showcasing successful implementations in similar environments.
**Metric:** Number of unique visitors to educational content.

## Consider
**Trigger Event:** Initial research into available solutions.
**Friction Points:**
- Difficulty in finding relevant information about laser-guidance and inertial measurement units.
- Skepticism about the reliability and accuracy of alternative navigation methods.
**User Emotions:** Skepticism, hope, confusion.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Detailed product comparisons highlighting the advantages of the laser-drone-target.
- Webinars or demo videos showcasing the product in action.
**Metric:** Number of downloads of product comparison guides.

## Try
**Trigger Event:** Decision to test the laser-drone-target solution.
**Friction Points:**
- Complexity in setting up and integrating the solution with existing systems.
- Concerns about the learning curve for new technology.
**User Emotions:** Excitement, anxiety, anticipation.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Free trial or pilot program with dedicated support.
- Comprehensive onboarding materials and training sessions.
**Metric:** Number of free trial sign-ups and completion rates.

## Adopt
**Trigger Event:** Successful trial and decision to purchase.
**Friction Points:**
- Budget constraints and procurement processes.
- Integration challenges with existing infrastructure.
**User Emotions:** Relief, satisfaction, confidence.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Customized implementation support and consulting services.
- Regular updates and improvements based on user feedback.
**Metric:** Conversion rate from trial to paid subscription.

## Expand
**Trigger Event:** Positive experience with the initial adoption.
**Friction Points:**
- Scaling the solution across multiple departments or units.
- Ensuring consistent performance and reliability.
**User Emotions:** Confidence, pride, ambition.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Expanded feature sets and additional modules.
- Partnership opportunities for joint development and innovation.
**Metric:** Number of additional departments or units adopting the solution.
