## 1. Problem Statement
Drone operators frequently face environments where GPS signals are denied, jammed, or unreliable (e.g., indoor warehouses, dense urban canyons, underground infrastructure, or conflict zones). In these GPS-denied scenarios, standard waypoint navigation and location tagging fail. Operators currently lack a streamlined, intuitive tool to visually identify and log specific targets of interest using onboard optical and laser sensors, leading to missed data points and increased cognitive load during manual flight.

## 2. Target Users
*   **Industrial Inspectors:** Inspecting infrastructure in GPS-denied environments (bridges, tunnels, mines).
*   **Search & Rescue (SAR) Teams:** Locating subjects or hazards in dense canopy or indoor rubble.
*   **Defense/Security Operators:** Identifying threats or points of interest in electronic warfare environments where GPS is spoofed.

## 3. Goals
### 3.1 Business Goals
*   Provide a lightweight, revenue-viable tool for the specific niche of GPS-denied operations.
*   Establish a foothold in the drone telemetry/mission software market by solving a specific pain point (targeting without global coordinates).

### 3.2 User Goals
*   **Precision:** Accurately mark a target location
