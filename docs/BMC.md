# Business Model Canvas - laser-drone-target

## Value Proposition
- Enables precise ground‑target designation using laser designation without GPS, effective in GPS‑denied or contested environments.  
- Intuitive UI (web‑based or mobile) lets operators point, lock, and adjust targets in real time with minimal training.  
- Reduces collateral damage, improves mission effectiveness for defense, law‑enforcement, and commercial aerial inspection.  
- Open‑source core with optional commercial support, low latency, and rugged design for field deployment.

## Customer Segments
- Defense contractors & military units (special forces, artillery, UAV operators) needing GPS‑independent targeting.  
- Law enforcement & SWAT teams for hostage/rescue or tactical operations.  
- Commercial aerial inspection firms (oil & gas, power lines, infrastructure) operating in areas with unreliable GPS.  
- Research institutions developing laser‑based targeting or counter‑UAS technologies.  
- Hobbyist/prosumer drone builders (secondary market, low‑touch licensing).

## Channels
- Direct sales via Axentx Business Development team to defense and government contracts.  
- OEM partnerships with drone manufacturers (DJI, Autel, Parrot) for bundled hardware‑software kits.  
- Online self‑serve portal (Axentx internal store) for software licenses and support plans.  
- Technical demos, documentation, and video tutorials hosted on GitHub/GitLab.  
- Trade shows and industry events (AUSA, Interdrone, DEF CON) for lead generation and partnership building.

## Revenue Streams
- Annual software license fee per drone platform or per operator seat.  
- Tiered support & maintenance contracts (standard, premium, enterprise).  
- Professional services for custom integration, calibration, and mission‑specific tuning.  
- Add‑on analytics package (post‑mission targeting accuracy reports, heat‑maps).  
- Government grants / SBIR funding for advanced R&D phases.

## Cost Structure
- Personnel: software engineers, robotics experts, UX/UI designers, systems engineers (Axentx OS roles).  
- Hardware prototyping: laser diodes, gimbals, ruggedized enclosures, test drones.  
- Cloud & simulation infrastructure (AWS GovCloud/Azure) for hardware‑in‑the‑loop testing and data storage.  
- Compliance & certification costs (ITAR, MIL‑STD, export controls).  
- Marketing & sales: BD travel, trade‑show fees, promotional material.  
- Legal/IP: patent filing, licensing, counsel.  
- Customer support, training, and documentation maintenance
